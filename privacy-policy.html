Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

LuckyDreams processes everything 100% on your device. Your dreams never leave your phone. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. LuckyDreams does not require registration, email addresses, or any form of account creation.

  • Dream text and recordings: Processed entirely on your device using a local AI model. Never transmitted to any server.
  • Dream history: Stored locally on your device using on-device storage. Not accessible to us or any third party.
  • Voice input: Processed using Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio data is not stored or transmitted.

2. Data That Stays on Your Device

All of the following data remains exclusively on your device:

  • Dream descriptions you enter or speak
  • Oracle interpretations generated by the local AI model
  • Dream history and statistics
  • App preferences and settings
  • Achievement progress

3. Third-Party Services

LuckyDreams uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — For subscription management. RevenueCat processes your purchase through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Sentry — For crash reporting and error monitoring. Sentry may collect anonymous crash data (stack traces, device type, OS version) to help us improve app stability. No personal information or dream content is included. Sentry's privacy policy is available at sentry.io/privacy.

We do not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or user-tracking tools.

4. No Tracking

LuckyDreams does not:

  • Track your location
  • Use cookies or tracking pixels
  • Collect device identifiers
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Perform any form of user profiling

5. Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Use the Export function in the Ledger screen to save your dream history as a JSON file.
  • Delete: Long-press any dream entry to delete it, or use Clear All to remove all history. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data.

6. Children's Privacy

LuckyDreams is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
