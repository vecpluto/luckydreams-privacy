# Privacy Policy for LuckyDreams

**Last Updated: March 8, 2026**

## Introduction

LuckyDreams ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Dream Descriptions**: Text you enter for dream interpretations. This data is processed locally on your device using an on-device AI model and is never transmitted to our servers.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Purchase Information**: If you subscribe to our premium service, purchase transactions are processed by Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information directly.
- **Analytics Data**: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data related to your subscription status. For more information, see [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/).

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide dream interpretation services via on-device AI processing
- To manage your subscription status
- To improve our app and services

## Data Storage and Security

- **On-Device Processing**: All dream interpretation is performed locally on your device using an embedded AI model. Your dream text is never sent to external servers.
- **No Account Required**: LuckyDreams does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the free tier.
- **Subscription Data**: Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store infrastructure.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Apple App Store | Payment processing | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/) |

## Children's Privacy

LuckyDreams is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Use the app without providing personal information (free tier)
- Cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store
- Request information about data associated with your device

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
